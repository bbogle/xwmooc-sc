These checklists are meant to help everyone involved in a workshop have a smoother, more rewarding experience. Please send us fixes and additions so that we can help people better.

  • Administrators: Software Carpentry staff who help set things up.
  • Hosts: people on site handling local arrangements.
  • Instructors: teaching part or all of the lessons.
  • Lead Instructors: responsible for coordinating other instructors as well as teaching.
  • Helpers: assisting instructors during lessons, but not actually teaching.
  • Learners: the people we are trying to help.
